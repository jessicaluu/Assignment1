I'm currently a senior at Georgia State University with a concentration in Graphics and Human-Computer Interaction. I hope to pursue a career that would allow me reach my potential in the Front-End side of software or web development. I love computer science, but I also have a knack for digital art and photography. I work very well with computational mathematics. However, I believe that there's more than just allowing the computer to add numbers. There is also a visual aspect that pulls everything together. Computer science is fun, but art really is a strong passion of mine, and I would love to incorporate art into the computer science field as much as possible. The idea of technology is very interesting and conceptual, but if you add a dab of color and animation, it gives it a touch of abstraction and appeal.
